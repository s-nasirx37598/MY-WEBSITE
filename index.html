Solar System

The solar system includes the Sun and everything that orbits it: the eight planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune) and their moons, dwarf planets, asteroids, and comets. It is located in the Milky Way galaxy, and its objects are held together by the Sun's gravitational pull. The solar system has a distinct inner region with rocky, terrestrial planets and an outer region with gas and ice giants.
